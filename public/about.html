About Nekojin Interactive

Building worlds
✦ Nekojin Interactive LLC

One creator.
Two admins.
Infinite worlds.

I write stories about people trapped in worlds I built, then I build those worlds in Godot to see if they actually work.

Nekojin Interactive is a solo studio, but inside the fiction, it's a digital consciousness preservation system run by two administrators: Tama and Saki. They've suppressed their own memories to experience mortality alongside the souls they protect.

Every book and every game is part of the same universe. The stories are told from the inside.
